About Us

Our mission is simple: to harness the power of Artificial Intelligence to protect our planet from the devastating effects of wildfires.

🌍 Who We Are

We are a passionate team of engineers with a common goal — to detect wildfires early using advanced satellite imagery and AI.

This project was born out of a deep concern for the increasing frequency of wildfires around the world and the need for smarter, faster detection tools.

🚀 Our Vision

We're building more than just a wildfire detection tool — we're building a future where smart technology supports disaster prevention.

  • 🌐 Real-time wildfire alerts
  • 📡 Integration with local emergency services
  • 📊 Data-driven insights and analytics

🔥 What's Next?

We are currently working on features like live updates, automatic alert systems, and mobile integration to make our tool even more effective in real-world scenarios.
